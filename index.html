About Drashya

Drashya design studio is firm that approaches each project with a highly individual creative strategy and artistic sense of aesthetics that go beyond conventional design solutions.

Each exceptional design solution is an original and always mindful of the client's taste and needs. Each object within the space is carefully chosen or custom made. The firm's meticulously crafted interiors surprise and delight with dramatic contrasts — in colors, textures, shapes - always in harmony with the whole composition.

The full-service design practice specializes in complete renovations and build-outs, offering a comprehensive design journey
